AI-powered assistant

Take advantage of Gmail's intuitive AI-powered tools to streamline your email communication. Analyzing the content of incoming emails, Smart Reply automatically suggests words and phrases to complete your sentences, enabling you to respond with a single click. You can also choose to personalize Smart Compose suggestions based on your writing style, ensuring that the suggestions align with your preferred language and tone.

To begin using Smart Reply, simply open an incoming email in your Gmail Web App. The feature will automatically suggest three suitable responses based on the content of the received email. You can then select the response that best fits your intended message, and send it with a single click. Whether you're confirming the receipt of a document, accepting a meeting request, or responding to a professional inquiry, Smart Reply tailors its suggestions to the context of your email.
